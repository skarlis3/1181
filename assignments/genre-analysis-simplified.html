Genre Analysis Essay — Simplified Instructions

Use this guide alongside the full assignment page. It breaks the essay into smaller steps.

Your essay answers one big question:
What are the patterns in this type of post? Why do they matter to the community?
Or: What makes this type of post this type of post?

Important Words

WordWhat It Means
GenreA type or style of post
Discourse communityA group of people with a shared interest. They talk to each other online or in person.
ConventionsInformal rules. The things most posts of this type have in common.
PurposeWhy people make this type of post
AudienceWho reads, watches, or responds to these posts
FunctionHow this type of post helps the community

Essay Map

Your essay has this shape. Start wide, go narrow, then go wide again:

1. The Community (broad)
▼
2. Describe the Genre + Purpose + Audience
▼
3. Convention #1
▼
4. Convention #2
▼
5. Convention #3 (most specific)
▼
6. How This Type of Post Helps the Community
▼
7. Conclusion (broad again)

Write Your Essay in This Order

🌐 Section 1: Introduce the Community (1 paragraph)

Write about your online community.

  • Where does it exist? (Reddit, Facebook, TikTok, Discord, etc.)
  • What is the community about?
  • What kinds of posts do people make there?
  • Which one type of post will your essay be about?

🔍 Section 2: Describe + Purpose + Audience (1–2 paragraphs)

Describe this type of post. Then answer:

  • Purpose (why): Why do people make this type of post? What does it do?
  • Audience (who): Who sees these posts? Can anyone understand them? Or do you need to be part of the community?
What is a convention (informal rule)? A convention is something most posts of this type have in common. Look at your 3 samples. What do you see again and again? Those are the conventions.

Examples: how the post looks, what language or tone is used, what the post includes (images, links, videos, captions), how long it is.

🔴 Section 3: Convention (Informal Rule) #1 — (1 paragraph)

Pick ONE convention (informal rule). Write about it.

  • 1 Name it. What is the informal rule?
  • 2 Show examples. Use quotes or descriptions from your 3 samples.
  • 3 Explain why it matters. Why do creators do this? How does it help the post?

🟡 Section 4: Convention (Informal Rule) #2 — (1 paragraph)

Pick a SECOND convention (informal rule). Same steps:

  • 1 Name it.
  • 2 Show examples.
  • 3 Explain why it matters.

🟢 Section 5: Convention (Informal Rule) #3 — (1 paragraph)

Pick a THIRD convention (informal rule). Same steps:

  • 1 Name it.
  • 2 Show examples.
  • 3 Explain why it matters.

🤝 Section 6: How This Type of Post Helps the Community (1 paragraph)

Explain what this type of post does for the community.

  • Does it connect people? How?
  • What do people write in the comments?
  • What does this type of post show about what the community cares about?

📋 Section 7: Conclusion (1 short paragraph)

Write a short summary. Why is this type of post important to the community?

📎 Your 3 Samples (Evidence)

You need 3 examples of the same type of post from your community. All 3 must be the same type. Save this information for each one:

  • Creator name or username
  • Date posted
  • Link to the post
  • Short description of the post

Final Checklist

  • My essay is about one community
  • My essay is about one type of post (one genre)
  • I explain the purpose (why people make these posts)
  • I explain the audience (who reads or watches)
  • I analyze 3 conventions (informal rules) with examples
  • Each convention paragraph: names it, shows examples, explains why it matters
  • I explain how this type of post helps the community
  • I have 3 samples as evidence
  • I include a Works Cited page with links to my samples (MLA format)
  • My essay is 1,300–1,700 words
  • MLA format
