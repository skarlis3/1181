Self-Evaluation and Reflection Form

For this project, you will also complete a reflection and self-evaluation form to reflect on your writing process and evaluate your work. Complete this assignment once you have completed your revised draft.

Complete Before Your Conference

You must complete this self-evaluation and reflection before your individual conference with me. We will use your responses as a starting point for discussing your revised draft for Project 1.

Requirements

The requirements for the self-evaluation and reflection are located on the form itself in Canvas.

Submission

Complete this form on Canvas. The form is set up as a "quiz" so that you can fill it out directly in Canvas.

Grading

This assignment is graded on a credit/no credit basis. You will receive full credit for completing the form thoughtfully and thoroughly.
