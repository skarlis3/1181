Genre Remix: Recipe Edition

1. Genre Detective

Open the recipe sample. As a team, list every convention you notice:

  • Structure & organization
  • Formatting & layout
  • Tone & word choice
  • Visual & design elements

2. Find More Samples

Look up real recipes online. What's consistent across them? What varies?

3. Pick a Topic

Choose a "recipe" for something that is not food:

A Perfect Nap A Best Friend Procrastination The Perfect Playlist Surviving Finals

4. Design & Write It

On a blank piece of paper, design and write your recipe as a team.

  • Title, headnote, time/yield, ingredients, steps, tip
  • Match the tone & style of a real recipe
  • Design the layout on the page
  • Draw boxes for images (e.g. "photo of a cozy blanket")

5. Gallery Walk

Put your recipe up on the wall. We'll walk around and see everyone's remixes.
